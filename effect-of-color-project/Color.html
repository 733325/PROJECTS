color in terms of websites is a intergral part of website design that goes beyond the actual navigation, functionality, and internal code of a website. Color allows you to almost directly choose how you want users to feel and percieve your website. With a person who knows how to manipulte colors they can drastically change the amount of users that stay on the website after the first click. you only need to look at professional to understand this effect such as the websites listed before.

The same can also be applied the opposite way with a person who has no idea what they are doing and choosing random colors being able to make a fundamentaly good website into one that can't keep many users after the first click. This can be shown with a website demostrated in the design concept I have created. The importance of the use of good colors and implementation is vital to any successful website
